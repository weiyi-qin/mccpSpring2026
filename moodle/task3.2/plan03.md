### Key Requirements

**Assignment:** Write an Introduction and a Literature Review of your research

**Length:** 1000-1500 words (excluding citations and annotations)

**Structure:**

#### INTRODUCTION (3 Moves)
1. **Move 1 – Establishing a Territory**
   - Present the general topic area/research problem
   - Highlight the importance of the research
   - Synthesise the key prior research that supports the need for the current research

2. **Move 2 – Identifying a Niche**
   - Introduce opposing viewpoints/perspectives to the current state of knowledge
   - Identify gaps in the existing literature
   - Explain why addressing these gaps is necessary

3. **Move 3 – Occupying the Niche**
   - State your research purpose or question
   - Outline the objectives of your research and how it will fill the gaps

#### LITERATURE REVIEW (4 Moves)
1. **Move 1 – Thematic Overview**
   - Provide context (defining key terms/concepts/research teams)
   - Explain the purpose and scope of the review

2. **Move 2 – Critical Analysis**
   - Group literature according to common themes, paradigms, theories, methodologies, or chronological order
   - Summarize and synthesize existing literature
   - Show connections, relationships, or contrasts among studies
   - Critique methodologies and findings of previous research

3. **Move 3 – Research Gaps**
   - Reiterate the gaps identified in previous studies
   - Emphasize the need for further investigation

4. **Move 4 – Conclusion of the Literature Review**
   - Summarize key insights and their implications
   - Set the stage for your research contribution
   - Emphasize the significance of the literature in relation to your research question

### Assessment Criteria

Your writing will be assessed on three dimensions:

**1. Task Achievement** (focus for this task)
- Extremely clear explanation of research background, focus, and objectives
- Skilful paraphrase, synthesis, and critical evaluation of relevant literature
- Clearly delineated research gap
- Succinctly highlighted significance of research
- Adequate and effective use of sources, proper citations & referencing

**2. Organisation**
- Logical flow of ideas and effective use of cohesive devices
- Clear paragraph structure and topic development
- Coherent research narrative

**3. Language Range and Accuracy**
- Effective use of advanced grammatical structures
- Precise lexical resources and word choice
- Accurate spelling, punctuation, and word forms

**Note:** For this task, we focus primarily on **Task Achievement**, but the other criteria are also important for your final assignment. See `writing_instructions_formatted.md` for complete rubric details.

**Action:** Read `writing_instructions_formatted.md` completely to understand all requirements, formatting, and assessment criteria.
